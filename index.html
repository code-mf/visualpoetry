Breathe

A calming breathing exercise with synchronized brown noise and visual feedback.

Breathe Installation

Create and manage multiple breathing circles. Click to add/remove, hover and scroll to scale. Press spacebar to toggle black/white theme.

Hours

A full-screen responsive grid of circles showing the hours remaining in the day. Filled circles represent hours yet to come, while empty circles show hours that have passed.

Minutes

A real-time visualization of the remaining minutes in the current hour. 60 circles in a grid, with filled circles showing minutes yet to pass.
